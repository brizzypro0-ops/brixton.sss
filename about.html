About Our School

Brixton Senior Secondary School is dedicated to providing quality education that nurtures creativity, discipline, leadership, and moral integrity among students. Since its establishment, the school has maintained a strong reputation for academic excellence and holistic development.

Our History

Brixton Senior Secondary School was established in 2006 by Mr. Sentamu Harunah with the aim of providing quality education and nurturing responsible citizens. Over the years, the school has grown into a center of academic excellence and discipline. This success has been driven by consistent supervision, well-trained teachers, and the dedication of our beloved students.

Brixton is founded on Islamic principles, ensuring that religious values are upheld while promoting inclusivity. Students from all religious backgrounds are welcomed and respected, fostering unity and harmony within the school community.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education that promotes academic excellence, discipline, innovation, and moral values, preparing students for future challenges.

Our Vision

To become a leading secondary school producing responsible, creative, and globally competitive students.
