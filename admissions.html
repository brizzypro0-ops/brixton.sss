Admissions

Brixton Senior Secondary School welcomes students who are committed to academic excellence and discipline.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed primary education certificate
  • Copy of previous school report
  • Passport size photographs
  • Birth certificate copy

Application Procedure

  1. Collect the application form from the school office.
  2. Fill the form with correct information.
  3. Submit the form together with required documents.
  4. Wait for admission confirmation.
